Keyboard Controls:
-------------------

[SHIFT] + Arrow keys: Move the map

Mouse Controls:
-------------------

Left mouse click: Will add the block selected in the "Add blocks" radio button group beneath the mouse pointer.
Right mouse click: Will delete the block beneath the mouse pointer.

-------------------
The text in the right/bottom of the screen shows the mouse its coordinates in block size.

To change the mapsize change the x_width & y_width values in the "Map specification" Section and press the "Ok" button beneath it.
