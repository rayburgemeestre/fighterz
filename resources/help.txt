#MAPMAKER CONTROLS#

[shift] + Arrow keys = move across the map

[left mouse]         = adds blocks or images at the mouse 
                       location according to the selected 
                       radio button in the "Add blocks" radio 
                       group.

[right mouse]        = Deletes only blocks at the mouse
                       pointers location, (deleting bg-images
                       has to go via the menu on your right).

[F12]                = Exit.

#INFO#

The text in the right/bottom of the screen shows the mouse its coordinates in block size.

To change the mapsize change the x_width & y_width values in the "Map specification" Section and press the "Ok" button.
